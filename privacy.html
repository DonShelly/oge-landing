3. How and Why We Use Your Data

Business-to-business outreach

We contact employees at corporate subscribers (limited companies, LLPs, and PLCs registered in the UK) to introduce our services. Under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), unsolicited emails to corporate subscribers are permitted without prior consent.

Lawful basis: Legitimate Interests (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

Legitimate Interests Assessment

We have conducted a Legitimate Interests Assessment to ensure our outreach activities are proportionate and respectful of your rights:

  • Purpose: Growing our business through direct B2B outreach to relevant decision-makers
  • Necessity: Email is necessary to reach business decision-makers who may benefit from our services
  • Balancing: We only contact individuals in a professional context, our emails are relevant to their role, and we provide an easy way to opt out. We believe the recipient's interests do not override our legitimate interests.

Service delivery

If you become a client, we process your data to deliver our services, manage our relationship, and communicate with you.

Lawful basis: Contract performance (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(b))

4. Email Tracking

Our emails may contain a small transparent image (tracking pixel) and tracked links. These allow us to understand whether emails are delivered, opened, and whether links are clicked. This data helps us improve the relevance of our communications and respect your preferences.

You can prevent tracking pixel loading by disabling images in your email client. You can opt out of all further communications at any time (see Section 6).

6. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

Opt out

Reply "STOP" to any email, click the unsubscribe link, or email us. We process opt-outs within 24 hours.

Access your data

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correct your data

Ask us to update inaccurate or incomplete data.

Delete your data

Request erasure of your personal data (subject to legal obligations).

Object to processing

Object to our use of your data under legitimate interests.

Complain

Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

To exercise any of these rights: email adrian@elsolve.co.uk or reply "STOP" to any email from us. We will respond within 30 days.
